What are Matchups in Smash

A matchup is how well any two characters do against each other. With a cast of 89 fighters, this equates to nearly 8,000 unique matchups that can happen in the game. Palutena vs Wolf is different to Samus vs Donkey Kong, but of course Mega Man vs Sora will bring something new to the table the same way that PAC-MAN vs Duck Hunt throw something new into the ring.

In Smash, how good a matchup is can be determined with a score that ranges from -3 to +3. +3 matchups are very rare in the game, and they are so disproportionately favoured to one character that, if played well, the one piloting the favoured character should, in paper, never lose. Most are within -1 to +1 though, one character will generally either be even or have a slight favour, but not so that it can't be overcome with good skill.
